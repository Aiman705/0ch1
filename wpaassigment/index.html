Help User Health Care

Health awareness is about understanding and improving your well-being through informed decisions and actions. It involves knowing the importance of maintaining a healthy lifestyle, staying informed about potential health risks, and actively engaging in preventive measures to ensure a longer, healthier life.

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a measure used to determine if you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese based on your height and weight. It's a useful tool for assessing whether you are in a healthy weight range and can help guide you towards making better lifestyle choices.
